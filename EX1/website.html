Musical Lights

Description of Product

Ever been playing your instrument and thought to yourself 'man, I need some new, something exciting, something to change the way I play forever!' Well if you have todays your lucky day as we have something that can change the way you play forever! Introducing our Musical Lights, leds that connect to your instruments and everytime you play different notes different colors will begin to flash! Time to turn those boring shred sessions into new invigorating ones!

Each note with be color coordinated with certain colors that you may be able to even choose yourself! This product could help children with learning notes with color or just make a jam session more enjoyable. Either way Musical Lights are coming soon!
